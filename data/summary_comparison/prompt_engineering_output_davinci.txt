

Key Differences between 2015 Text and 2023 Text:
- 2015 Text: 
    - Focuses on the iTunes Store Service 
    - Governs the use of the iTunes Store Service, Mac App Store, App Store, and iBooks Store
    - Only available to users in the UK
    - Users must be 13 years or older
- 2023 Text: 
    - Focuses on Apple's Services such as the App Store, Apple Music, Apple TV, and others
    - Governs the use of Apple's Services
    - Availability of services and content may vary by country
    - Users must be at least 13 years old to create accounts
